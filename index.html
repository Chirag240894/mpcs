We are MPCS

Unit is registered as a Rahul S. Mithaiwala & Co. Practising company secretaries firm with the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI).

  • MPCS ASSOCIATES LLP is dealing with matters relating to Company Law, ROC, NCLT, Insolvency, Mergers/compromise/arrangement, Securities Laws, FEMA, Legal Due Diligence.
  • Along with these we also deal with Income Tax, Goods and Service Tax and Other Taxation matters, Project Finance, RERA services.

COMPANY LAW MATTERS

Clients frequently require professional assistance with many aspects of the business during its start up phase. Commencing a new business of any size requires diligent and strategic planning, including a thorough risk benefit analysis to assess future profitability.

Our services in this area include :

  • Company Incorporation
  • Company Law Advisory Services on proposed corporate actions
  • Issue based consultancy to Large scale Companies and Listed Companies
  • Services of a Company Secretary on retainer basis to small and medium scale companies for which mandatory appointment of Company Secretary is not required.
  • Drafting statutory documents
  • Liaison with regulatory authorities’ viz. Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Registrar of Companies, Regional Director, to obtain clearances and approvals under the Companies Act, 2013.
  • Amalgamation, Merger and Corporate Restructuring
  • Due diligence of target company.
  • Voluntary Winding-up and striking-off the name of Company
  • Petitions to Regional Director for Compounding of Offences
  • Petitions to National Company Law Tribunal
  • Secretarial Audit as per Section 204 of the Companies Act 2013 for bigger Companies
  • Creating Corporate Governance Environment for unlisted Companies
  • Initial Public Offer (IPO) or Public Issue related consultancy and support services

SERVICES UNDER FOREIGN EXCHANGE MANAGEMENT ACT (FEMA)

We advise on various foreign exchange matters under the Act including in connection with that stemming from inbound investment into India and outbound investment outside India. We also help the client to obtain various approvals under the said Act from the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) and review of various compliances prescribed by the RBI.

Our services in this area include :

  • Consultation and Advisory Services
  • Setting up of Joint Venture Company in India
  • Setting up of Wholly Owned Subsidiaries and Joint Venture Companies Abroad.
  • Drafting of foreign collaboration / joint venture / technology transfer agreement etc.
  • Liaison with Reserve Bank of India / Secretariat of Industrial Approvals / Foreign Investment Promotion Board etc. and obtaining necessary approvals
  • Permissions for External Commercial Borrowings
  • Obtaining various permissions from RBI under FEMA
